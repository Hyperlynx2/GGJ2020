"A well maintained series of deadly traps provides a great look and feel to any dungeon and increases the appeal of your property."

Become a franchisee of Tim's Dungeon Repair today! Be your own boss, with challenging work in the Great Indoors. You'll be resetting and re-arming a host of deadly traps, but be sure not to set them off again accidentally on your way out!

Controls: WASD/arrows + space.


Credits: Ronald Chau, Leonard Frankel, and Jarrod Newberry, with audio by Gareth Wiecko.
